Welcome to The Godfather fanpage!!

(Where I talk about mostly just the first movie)

Ever since my dad showed me this series, I've been so entranced by the film's history and behind the scenes story. Everything from the origin of the story, to the family that created the film, and all the challenges the production of the film was involved in, I'm just fascinated by. This iconic trilogy began as an adaptation of Mario Puzo's book published in 1969. It was directed by Francis Ford Coppola starring Al Pacino, and this website will be focusing on the first film of the trilogy The Godfather (1972).
