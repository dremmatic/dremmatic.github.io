Top 3 things in school

At Eastside Preparatory School there are many things to be happy about. Today, we are counting down the top 3 things at Eastside Prep. These are the activities, people, and things me, and many others are extremely happy to have at school.

    1

    Food

    Eastside Preparatory School has a wide variety of choices for food. Different desserts including mini churros, mini donust, and many different cakes. The entrees, though, are different than any other school. Examples include teriyaki beef, loaded baked potato, and even filet mignon.

    2

    Teachers

    At Eastside Prep, every single teacher you will meet has a wonderful and outstanding personality. They are all so kind and helpful and will help you with anything you need.

    3

    Sports

    One of our claims to fame at Eastside Preparatory School is our sports. Over the last few years, our strongest sport has been Ultimate Frisbee, in which we have placed highly in national championships multiple times.
