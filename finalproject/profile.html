HIROSHI NAGAI

C A R E E R _ T I M E L I N E




1947

Born in Tokushima City

1970

Works as a graphic designer

1973

40 days trip to America. In the United States, I purchased about 100 soul music records, which would form the basis of my record collection.

1974

Travel to Guam Island. He was so moved by the palm jungle that he made it his life's work.

1975

Under the guidance of Teruhiko Yumura, he aims to become an illustrator (while also raising funds to purchase records).

1978

Becomes a freelance illustrator.

1979

Summer picture book [A LONG VACATION] (written by Eiichi Otaki) is released by CBS Sony Publishing.

1981

[HALATION] (written by Tsuko Kuri) is released by CBS Sony Publishing. .

1997

Released compilation CD [SUMMER MADNESS] (song selection, jacket & calendar illustration) from AVEX TRAX.

1999

Hiroshi Nagai's new work exhibition [SUMMER MADNESS] will be held at Shibuya Bunkamura Gallery.

2002

Victor Entertainment has released an AOR compilation CD [breeze SUMMER - AOR best selection] (jacket illustration) and a DVD collection [breeze presents Hiroshi Nagai's summer madness] (jacket illustration).

2003

Victor Entertainment releases AOR compilation CD [breeze COOL SUMMER - AOR best selection] (jacket illustration).
